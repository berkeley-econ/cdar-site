2023-03: Lack of Compactness of Budget Sets in Infinite Dimensional Commodity Spaces and Its Consequences

Konstantin Magin
2023

This paper demonstrates that the budget sets in the Contingent Markets (CM) and Financial Markets (FM) economies with infinite dimensional commodity spaces might not be norm-bounded and therefore might not be weakly (weakly*) compact. The lack of weak (weak*) compactness of these budget sets has serious implications. In particular, it is no longer guaranteed that weakly (weakly*) continuous utility functions attain their maximum on these budget sets.
